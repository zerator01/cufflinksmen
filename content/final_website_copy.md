## 6. FAQ — Orders & Product (B2C)

1. **Q: What materials are your cufflinks made of?**
   A: It depends on the pair. Our entry-level cufflinks use zinc alloy with enamel or glass detailing. Our mid-range pairs feature gold-plated or silver-plated brass. Our premium pairs use solid stainless steel or 925 sterling silver. Every product page lists the exact materials — no guesswork.

2. **Q: What mechanism types do you carry?**
   A: We carry three main types: bullet-back (the most common — a small cylinder that rotates to lock), whale-back (a flat toggle — sleek and modern), and fixed-back (no moving parts — the most durable). Each product page specifies the mechanism type.

3. **Q: Do you offer free shipping?**
   A: Yes — free worldwide shipping on all orders over $35. Orders under $35 have a flat shipping rate shown at checkout.

4. **Q: How long does delivery take?**
   A: Most orders ship within 1-3 business days. Standard delivery takes 7-15 business days depending on your location. Tracking is included with every order.

5. **Q: Can I track my order?**
   A: Absolutely. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

6. **Q: What's your return policy?**
   A: We offer a 30-day return policy. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items should be unworn and in original packaging.

7. **Q: Will the gold plating wear off?**
   A: Let's be straightforward — gold plating is not solid gold. With daily wear, it will show signs of use over time. For weddings, events, and occasional formal wear, it holds up well. Store them in the included pouch and keep them away from water and cologne.

8. **Q: Will these fit my shirts?**
   A: Cufflinks are designed for French cuff (double cuff) shirts — the kind with two buttonholes instead of buttons. They won't attach to standard button-cuff shirts. If you're buying for a wedding, most rental tuxedo shirts already have French cuffs.
