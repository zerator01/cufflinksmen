### Built to Stay in Place All Night

A stud that rotates mid-dance is not a detail — it's a distraction. Each piece in this set uses a double-pin spring clutch mechanism: two parallel steel pins engage the shirt buttonhole simultaneously, distributing lateral pressure evenly so the stud sits flat against the fabric without rocking. The cuff links feature a traditional bar-and-toggle design with a friction-locked hinge. The 18mm (0.71 in) bar length is proportioned for a standard French cuff without overhang. At a total weight of 42g for the full set, the pieces are substantial enough to feel premium without pulling the shirt fabric.

### Care and Longevity

Lacquered brass requires minimal maintenance: wipe with a soft dry cloth after each wear to remove body oils and perspiration, which are the primary causes of dulling in unsealed metals. Store in the provided pouch or a compartmentalised case to prevent the onyx facets from scratching against harder metals. Avoid immersion in water, contact with chlorine pools, and aerosol hairspray applied directly — these break down the lacquer seal over time. With reasonable care, the finish will remain bright through years of formal occasions.
