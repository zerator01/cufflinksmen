### Solid Brass, Not Hollow

Many novelty cuff links are hollow stampings that dent at a touch. These are cast from solid brass — the same alloy used in quality hardware and marine fittings — giving each cuff link enough weight to feel substantial on the wrist without dragging. The dragon head face is approximately 14 mm × 14 mm: large enough to be clearly visible through a jacket sleeve, not so large that it overwhelms a classic French cuff.

The same dragon head silhouette is cast and then finished in your choice of gold-tone or silver-tone plating. Gold-tone reads as warm and authoritative — pair it with navy, burgundy, or cream shirts. Silver-tone is cooler and reads as more contemporary — it stands out crisply against black or charcoal. ### Toggle Backing That Actually Holds

The bullet-style toggle backing is the workhorse of quality cuff links. It pivots smoothly through the cuff hole and the extended toggle arm holds it securely — no twist, no slippage mid-handshake. The mechanism has been used in formal menswear for over a century because it works. Fits all standard double-cuff and convertible cuff shirt designs.
